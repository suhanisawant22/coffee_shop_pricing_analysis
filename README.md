# Coffee-Shop-Pricing-Analysis
## Project Objective
This project presents a comprehensive pricing & profitability Analysis for a Coffee Shop business using order data of the years 2023, 2024 and 2025. The dashboard evaluates Revenue, Profit, Quantity Sold and Gross Margin % (GMP) across multiple years to support pricing decisions. The dashboard identifies low-margin products and provides clear pricing recommendations to improve profitability while maintaining business sustainability.
## Business Problem 
- Significant decrease in overall profit margins due to rising cost of goods sold 
- Performance  variation across regions and products
## Goal of project 
- Identify products with Gross Margin % below 30%
- Provide year – over – year analysis
- Analyze profit by region , top products by revenue 
- Recommend price increase , continuation or discontinuation of products

## Business Insights & Recommendations

Chemex Filters (100 pack) with a total GMP of 16.31 % 
Minimalist Keychain with a total GMP of 16.35 %
Logo Hoodie (Black) with a total GMP of 16.61 %
Increase price for Gooseneck Electric Kettle to raise GMP above 30 %
